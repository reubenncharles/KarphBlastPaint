Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What is sandblasting and how does it work?

Sandblasting is a surface preparation technique that uses compressed air to propel abrasive media at high velocity against a surface. This effectively removes old paint, rust, corrosion, and other contaminants, leaving a clean surface ready for painting or coating.

Is sandblasting safe for all surfaces?

Different surfaces require different approaches. We use various media types and pressures depending on the material. For delicate surfaces, we may recommend gentler methods like soda blasting or very fine media. Our experts will assess your specific surface and recommend the most appropriate technique.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on size, complexity, and weather conditions. Small items might be completed in a day, while larger projects like industrial equipment or building exteriors can take several days to weeks. We'll provide a timeline estimate during our initial consultation.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work. We offer warranties on both our sandblasting and painting services. The specific warranty period depends on the project type and materials used. Details will be provided in your project quote.

How do you contain dust and debris during sandblasting?

We use professional containment systems including enclosures, dust collection equipment, and vacuum systems to minimize environmental impact. For indoor projects, we seal off the work area. Our processes comply with all environmental regulations.

What areas do you service?

Based in Schofields, we service the greater Sydney metropolitan area, including Blacktown, Parramatta, Penrith, Windsor, and surrounding suburbs. For larger projects, we may travel further - please contact us to discuss your location.
